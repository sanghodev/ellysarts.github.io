Arts & Crafts Class for Kids

Discover Elly's Arts & Crafts, the perfect arts & crafts experience for your kids.

We want to provide a warm space to encourage the growth and development of aspiring artists or designers. Our objective is to ensure that each student expands their understanding of visual art and techniques in a fun and fulfilling way that reflects their individual personality with creation of unique works through a variety of mediums, hands on projects, discussion and observation with both peers and experienced teachers.

Classes about Arts & Crafts

Drawing for Beginners

This class focuses on the basics of drawing with pencil, eraser and paper. Drawing increases focus for young developing minds.

Painting for Beginners

Hands on approach to different techniques which include watercolor, acrylics and multimedia with collage and oil pastels. Classes will introduce various painting styles, color theory, and highlight artists/illustrators which will aid in the growth of the students' work.

Crafts for Fun

A fun day to get your hands dirty and your mind to work. Inspired by holidays, history, or cultural crafts; it will be a day to let loose and have fun.

Arts Play Day

Get a couple of your friends together to make a piece of art! Draw, paint, craft together in a fun setting with a variety of supplies.
